1. Search Engine Optimization

SEO is a very important digital marketing strategy. The concept is simple, people often trigger searches on Search Engines like Google or Bing. These searches could be for some information only or some future sale prospect for a certain online business. Now, usually whosoever covers the top positions for popular searches tend to end up with having more customer thus more sales. SEO defines all the strategies for improving the online reach corresponding to various organic search queries through search engines. There are many factors like website speed and performance, relevant keywords, keyword optimization and relevant contents that improves the SEO for a website.

2. On & Off Page SEO

SEO needs to be conquered in two main fronts and that On Page and Off Page SEO techniques. On Page covers your website/app’s performance, speed, page optimization as in assigning the right titles and keywords specific to the pages. You can also various analytical tools to analyze the traffic trends and improvements. On the other hand, off page focuses more onto the online presence for the business-like relevant backlinks, business directories, social book markings, classifieds, content sharing and blogs. The more relevant imprints on the web for your business would eventually lead to better organic positions. VTS provide services for both On Page, off page and various SEO tools (SEMrush, ahrefs etc.) to support your website SEO.
